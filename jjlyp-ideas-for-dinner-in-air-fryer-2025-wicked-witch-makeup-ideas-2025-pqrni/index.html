Ideas for Dinner in Air Fryer 2025

The air fryer has become a popular kitchen appliance in recent years, and for good reason. It’s a versatile appliance that can be used to cook a variety of foods, from chicken to fish to vegetables. And because it uses hot air to cook food, it’s a healthier option than frying food in oil.

If you’re looking for some new ideas for dinner in the air fryer, here are a few recipes to get you started:

  • Air Fryer Chicken Wings: These are a classic party food, and they’re easy to make in the air fryer. Simply toss the chicken wings in your favorite sauce and cook them in the air fryer until they’re golden brown and crispy.
  • Air Fryer Salmon: Salmon is a healthy and delicious fish that’s perfect for cooking in the air fryer. Simply season the salmon with your favorite herbs and spices and cook it in the air fryer until it’s cooked through.
  • Air Fryer Roasted Vegetables: Roasted vegetables are a great way to get your daily dose of vegetables. Simply toss your favorite vegetables in olive oil and seasonings and cook them in the air fryer until they’re tender and slightly browned.
  • Air Fryer Pizza Rolls: These are a fun and easy snack or appetizer. Simply roll out some pizza dough, spread on your favorite sauce and toppings, and cook them in the air fryer until they’re golden brown and bubbly.
  • Air Fryer French Fries: French fries are a classic side dish, and they’re easy to make in the air fryer. Simply cut some potatoes into fries, toss them in olive oil and seasonings, and cook them in the air fryer until they’re golden brown and crispy.

These are just a few ideas for dinner in the air fryer. With a little creativity, you can use the air fryer to cook a variety of delicious and healthy meals.
